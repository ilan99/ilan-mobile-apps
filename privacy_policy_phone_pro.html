Privacy Policy

Phone Pro – Voice Control

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Ilan Mobile Apps (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Phone Pro – Voice Control mobile application (the “App”).

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

We do not operate servers, require user accounts, or directly collect information such as names, phone numbers, contacts, call logs, or audio.

Accessibility Service Usage

The App uses the Android Accessibility Service only to enable hands-free, voice-controlled call actions.

  • Detect incoming calls from supported calling apps
  • Announce the caller’s name aloud
  • Allow answering or declining calls using voice commands

Accessibility data is:

  • Not collected
  • Not stored
  • Not transmitted
  • Not shared with third parties

Default Phone App

The App may be set as the device’s default phone application to support voice-based call handling. The App does not collect or store call logs, call content, or contact data.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party SDKs that may automatically collect limited data as part of their normal operation. This data is collected directly by the SDK providers, not by us.

Google AdMob

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, and approximate location information for ad delivery and measurement.

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Firebase Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to measure app usage and performance. Firebase Analytics may collect anonymized and aggregated usage data and device information.

Firebase Crashlytics

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes and stability issues. Crash reports may include device information and app state at the time of a crash.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data. Any data collected by third-party SDKs is processed under their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Because the App does not directly collect or store data, data security risks are minimized. Third-party services apply their own security measures.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

User Choices

  • You may disable the Accessibility Service at any time in system settings
  • You may opt out of personalized ads via your device’s ad settings
  • You may stop using the App at any time

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
